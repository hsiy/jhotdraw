EventObject

Design Patterns

Observer
State changes of figures can be observed by other objects. Specifically

CompositeFigure

observes area invalidations and remove requests of its child figures. DrawingView also observes area invalidations of its drawing object. Subject: Figure; Observer: FigureListener; Event: FigureEvent; Concrete Observer: org.jhotdraw.draw.CompositeFigure, DrawingView.

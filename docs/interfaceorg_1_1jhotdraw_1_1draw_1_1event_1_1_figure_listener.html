Detailed Description

Interface implemented by observers of org.jhotdraw.draw.Figure objects.

Design Patterns

Observer
State changes of figures can be observed by other objects. Specifically

CompositeFigure

observes area invalidations and remove requests of its child figures.

DrawingView

also observes area invalidations of its drawing object. Subject: org.jhotdraw.draw.Figure; Observer: FigureListener; Event: FigureEvent; Concrete Observer: org.jhotdraw.draw.CompositeFigure, org.jhotdraw.draw.DrawingView.

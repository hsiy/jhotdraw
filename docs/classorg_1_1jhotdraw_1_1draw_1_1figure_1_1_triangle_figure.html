Detailed Description

Implements a Figure with a triangular shape.

The tip of the triangle points in the direction specified by the attribute org.jhotdraw.draw.AttributeKeys#ORIENTATION.

This figure creates a OrientationHandle which allows to interactively change the orientation of the triangle.
